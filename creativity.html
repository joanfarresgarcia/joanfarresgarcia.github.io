Learning to play the violin EXPERIENCE

After hearing a lot of classical music and violin pieces, such as La Campanella, an intense passion to learn the violin developed in me. As a result, in September, 2018 I started to play the violin. But I realized that one of my biggest flaws was inconsistency in practice times. I would practice a lot the few days before, but the during the whole week. This made it harder for me to improve more. What I try to do to find flaws in myself is look at myself from other points of view, without subjectivity.
