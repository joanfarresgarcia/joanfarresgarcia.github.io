Before 2017

Figure skating has been part of my life since I was 6 years old. It was through commitment and percervance that I've managed to win the spanish nationals and get 8th in American nationals in 2011.

However, due to injuries in my ankle, I had to slow my progress down and take long breaks from figure skating.

September, 2017

I was able to skate a little bit in September but, due to my injury getting worse, I had to stop and take a big break.

April, 2018

I went to skate but due to the injury I had to stop and couldn't do much. I decided to put more emphasis on the exercices that would grow mussles near the ankle to support it, since that is where my injury was most vurneable. I also got new skates so as to improve the stability of my ankle, though it only made it worse, as my feet would go in pain due to not being used to skating with the new skates.

November 16, 2018

I went to skate for a session and I was surprised at how much I missed figure skating. Although the main objective was to get slowly back into it, I was eager to start doing some jumps.

November 16, 2018

I went back to the rink to try to skate more during December, but I couldn't find a solution to my feet-pain due to new skates.
